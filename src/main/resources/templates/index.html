Migrate your account

Currently we are moving from the old portal to a new portal and your account has been selected for migration. the migration process should only take a few minutes and should let you start using the new portal straight away

To complete your account migration you need to;

  • Have a uniqu work email address if you do not have one you will create a new email address
  • Verify your account by a code sent to the new registered email address
  • Set up your display name and password
  • Activate your multi factor authentication for an added level of security

Once your account migration is confirmed you can start using the new portal

If you need any help you can contact the online support number 0208 3456789 or Online portal help

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

Before you start

You can also register by post.

The online service is also available in Welsh (ar gael yn Gymraeg).

You can’t register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.
